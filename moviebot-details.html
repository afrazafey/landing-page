MovieBot: Telegram-Powered Movie & Series Request System

MovieBot is a Telegram-integrated movie and series request platform built for modern content enthusiasts. It connects seamlessly with Sonarr, Radarr, and qBittorrent to automate downloading and library management.

Users can search IMDb, request titles, and admins can approve or reject requests directly from Telegram — all in real time. With instant notifications, image previews, and one-click downloads, MovieBot makes media management effortless.

Built for both speed and control, MovieBot is your all-in-one content delivery companion.
